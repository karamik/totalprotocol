### 2. THE MIND (Execution Layer - Sentinel Lite)

* **Formal Deadlock Verification:** A mathematical guarantee of zero-hang execution between the CPU and FPGA-accelerators.
* **Quantum Shield:** A hybrid signature scheme (ECDSA + Dilithium). Even in the event of a "Quantum Leap," assets remain secured by lattice-based primitives.

### 3. THE SPIRIT (Economic Layer - Entropy Anchor)
